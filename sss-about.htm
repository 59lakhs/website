59 LAKHS is in the process of forming a collective of creatives, SEX SYMBOL STUDIOS.

The goal of the collective is to showcase artists talented in various mediums who are currently flying under the radar.

We are building a community of BIPoC, Women, and LBGTQ+ creatives from Vancouver, Chicago, and the rest of the world with the intent of drawing widespread support for our underground artists.

By shooting, interviewing, and showing long-term support to artists whose values align with ours at SEX SYMBOL STUDIOS, we plan on pushing forward the music, art, fashion, and culture of the 2020s into a fresh and inclusive space.
